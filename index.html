Kabaddi World Cup 2025 Kicks Off in the UK

The 2025 Kabaddi World Cup has commenced in the Midlands, UK, marking the first time the tournament is being held outside Asia. From March 17 to 23, top male and female kabaddi players will compete in cities including Birmingham, Coventry, Walsall, and Wolverhampton. India enters the tournament as the strong favorite, aiming to defend their titles from the 2019 edition. Matches are being broadcast live and free via BBC iPlayer in the UK
